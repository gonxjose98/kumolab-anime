# KumoLab IG Operating Watchlist

**Locked 2026-07-17 · Fable analysis · re-check season rankings ~mid-August 2026**

The prioritized anime KumoLab should cover, blending "big global fanbase" (AniList popularity/trending, Anime Corner Summer 2026 poll, Crunchyroll lineup) with "KumoLab already performs here" (359-Reel history, see `IG-ANALYSIS-2026-07.md`). This is the operating list AND the seed for the pipeline's priority tiers.

**Cadence at 2-3 Reels/day:** one Tier 1 (guaranteed) + one Tier 2 (big-fanbase add) + a rotating Tier 3 slot. Preserves the TOHO moat (70% of all views) while capturing the franchises currently left on the table.

**Key correction (matters for automation):** the `source` channel in our data (e.g. "YouTube_Kadokawa", "YouTube_TOHO Animation") is the LABEL/producer, not the animation studio. Awajima Hyakkei is MADHOUSE, not a Kadokawa in-house show; Apothecary Diaries mainline is OLM. An AniList-based studio + popularity lookup is more accurate than keying off the source channel name.
