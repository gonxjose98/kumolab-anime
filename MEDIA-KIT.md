## Rate card rationale (provisional — your call)

Rates are anchored on **reach**, not follower count (1.6k IG followers undersells a page
doing hundreds of thousands of views/month). A dedicated Reel that reaches ~30–60k accounts
is worth materially more than a nano-influencer post; these are deliberately accessible
opening numbers to land the *first* deal, then raise once there's a case study.

| Offer | Opening rate | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Integrated placement (your news inside a KumoLab Reel) | from **$120** | Lowest-friction entry; uses the format that already performs |
| Dedicated sponsored Reel (custom, all platforms) | from **$250** | Full creative + fan-out to IG/FB/Threads |
| Newsletter feature | from **$80** | Owned audience, durable |
| Monthly bundle (3 Reels + newsletter) | from **$600** | Recurring = the MRR play; discount vs à la carte |

Raise these the moment one deal closes and becomes a reference. Bundles/campaigns/rev-share
are negotiable and should be, for a first partner.

## Outreach targets (10)

Ordered roughly easiest → hardest to land. Anime-adjacent brands that already spend on
creator/UGC promo and want fan-native reach.

| # | Target | Angle | Contact path |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Figure/collectible sellers** (Kotobukiya, Good Smile resellers, AmiAmi affiliates) | New-release figure drops shown to buyers | Affiliate/partnerships form; IG DM |
| 2 | **Anime apparel/streetwear brands** (indie, e.g. via IG) | Co-branded merch + Reel; barter-friendly | IG DM, founder email |
| 3 | **Mobile anime games** (gacha/RPG UA teams — HoYo-adjacent, Crunchyroll Games) | Launch/event pushes to engaged anime fans | UA agency; publisher biz-dev |
| 4 | **HIDIVE** | Simulcast season pushes; smaller/hungrier than Crunchyroll | Marketing contact; partnerships email |
| 5 | **Sentai / smaller distributors** | Home-video + streaming release promo | Marketing contact |
| 6 | **Anime convention circuits** (regional cons) | Ticket/lineup promo to local-ish fanbase | Sponsorship/press email |
| 7 | **Manga apps** (Manta, INKR, Tapas) | New-series spotlights | Partnerships form |
| 8 | **Anime subscription boxes** (Loot Anime-style) | Affiliate + unboxing Reel | Affiliate program |
| 9 | **Crunchyroll (creator/affiliate)** | Start via affiliate program, graduate to paid | Affiliate → partnerships |
| 10 | **Streaming/game studios direct** (TOHO, Aniplex titles) | Aspirational; lead with the breakout Reel case study | Cold, via the Reel proof |

**Tactic:** lead every pitch with the live media-kit link and one specific breakout Reel
(the numbers do the talking). Start with #1–#3 — they buy creator promo routinely and decide fast.
